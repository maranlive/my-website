Welcome

Welcome to the official webpage of Dr. A. Manimaran, an accomplished academic and researcher in the field of Data Science. Currently, he serves as an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) at VIT-AP University, playing a pivotal role in both teaching and research within the School of Advanced Sciences.

Dr. Manimaran has a strong academic foundation and has made significant contributions to diverse areas such as Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Time Series Analysis, and Generative AI. His scholarly work is well-recognized, with numerous publications in high-impact journals and conferences.

At VIT-AP University, Dr. Manimaran is committed to creating a dynamic learning environment, mentoring students, and driving innovative research to tackle real-world challenges in technology and science. His dedication to academic excellence and innovation inspires and shapes the next generation of engineers and researchers.
