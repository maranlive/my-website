Welcome to My Research Team

Our research group focuses on cutting-edge technologies in Data Science, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Time-Series Analysis. We strive for innovation through interdisciplinary collaborations and impactful research projects.

Aspiring Ph.D. scholars passionate about exploring next-generation technologies are encouraged to apply for guideship under my supervision. Together, we tackle challenging problems in data-driven sciences and create solutions that influence both academia and industry.

Research Areas:

  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Natural Language Processing and Text Analytics
  • Time-Series Forecasting and Data Mining
  • Generative AI and Advanced Model Development
